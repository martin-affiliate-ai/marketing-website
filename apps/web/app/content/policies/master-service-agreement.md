# Master Services Agreement (MSA)

This Master Services Agreement (the "Agreement") is made between **Affiliate Intelligence LTD** ("Affiliate.ai") and the **Advertiser** ("Advertiser"), and governs the Advertiser's use of Affiliate.ai's services and platform. Specific terms related to campaigns will be defined in Insertion Orders ("IOs").

## 1. Purpose

Affiliate.ai provides a performance marketing platform powered by AI agents. This Agreement governs the Advertiser's access to and use of the Affiliate.ai platform, tools, and network. Specific campaign terms will be outlined in associated IOs.

## 2. Definitions

- **Affiliate:** A third-party publisher participating in the Affiliate.ai network.
- **Commission:** The fee payable to an Affiliate for a Valid Transaction.
- **Override:** The service fee retained by Affiliate.ai in addition to Commission.
- **Valid Transaction:** A tracked conversion validated by the Advertiser within the validation window.
- **Tracking Period:** The period in which a tracked click can result in a valid transaction (default: 30 days).
- **Validation Period:** The timeframe within which the Advertiser must confirm or dispute transactions (default: 30 days).
- **MCP Agent:** An AI agent used to automate affiliate program operations such as validation, fraud review, and optimization.
- **IO:** A separately executed Insertion Order which sets out the commercial terms for a campaign.

## 3. Affiliate.ai Obligations

Affiliate.ai will provide:

- Access to the platform
- AI-powered tools for publisher management, tracking, and optimization
- Standard reporting and technical support
- Payment facilitation to Affiliates for Valid Transactions

## 4. Advertiser Obligations

The Advertiser must:

- Integrate accurate tracking
- Maintain accurate data
- Comply with applicable laws and regulations
- Manage disputes with Affiliates or customers

## 5. Tracking & Validation

- The Advertiser is responsible for maintaining functional tracking.
- If tracking fails due to the Advertiser's actions or inaction, Affiliate.ai may estimate conversions based on historical data.
- Transactions not validated within the Validation Period are auto-approved.

## 6. Data Protection

Both parties shall comply with applicable data protection laws including the UK GDPR. A separate Data Processing Agreement may be appended as Schedule 2.

## 7. Confidentiality
